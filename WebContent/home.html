Supernatural is the longest running tv fantasy and horror series ever. It started in 2005, created by Eric Kripke and starring Jensen Ackles and Jared Padalecki as Winchester brothers Dean and Sam, who travel the backroads of America in their 1967 black Chevy Impala, each week investigating and hunting down every demon, monster, ghost, and urban legend they can find, to a soundtrack of classic rock. It is broadcast on The CW, and the 11th season begins in October 2015.
